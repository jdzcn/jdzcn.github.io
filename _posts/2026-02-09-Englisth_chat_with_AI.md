一、最基础的聊天用语
这些是最常用的，几乎每次聊天都会用到。
How are you?
I’m good / I’m fine.
What’s up?
Not much.
How can I help you?
I need your help with …
Can you explain this?
What does this mean?
I don’t understand.
Could you say that again?
Sure. / Of course. / No problem.
二、问问题（和我聊天最常用）
这些是你每天都会用到的句型。
How do I …?
What is …?
Why is this happening?
How can I fix this?
Could you give me an example?
Could you explain it in simple terms?
What should I do next?
Is this correct?
三、表达你的想法
I think …
I feel …
In my opinion …
I agree. / I disagree.
I’m not sure.
Maybe. / Perhaps.
That makes sense.
I see.
四、让我重复、解释、简化
如果你听不懂，这些非常有用。
Could you repeat that?
Could you explain it again?
Could you use simpler words?
Could you give me more details?
What does “…” mean?
五、和学习相关的表达
I want to learn English.
I’m trying to improve my English.
Could you help me practice?
How can I remember this?
What’s the best way to learn …?
Could you correct my English?
六、软件 / 技术相关（你经常用）
I’m having a problem with …
There’s an error.
It doesn’t work.
How do I install this?
How do I configure …?
What version should I use?
七、感谢与结束对话
Thank you for your help.
I appreciate it.
That’s very helpful.
Have a nice day!
Talk to you later.
